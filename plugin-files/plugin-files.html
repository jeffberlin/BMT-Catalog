Wordpress Plugin

The wait is finally over! BMT Micro now offers a plugin for all of our vendors who are currently using, or wanting to use Wordpress for their website.

Installation:

  • Click the 'Download Plugin' button after the instructions section to download the .zip files (if it did not download as a .zip file, right click on the main folder and select "Compress").
  • Go to your Wordpress admin panel.
  • Click the "Plugins" tab (if you hover, select "Add New" and skip the next step).
  • At the top of the page next to the "Plugins" header, click the "Add New" button.
  • Click the "Upload Plugin" button.
  • Select the BMT Micro .zip/compressed file and then click Install.
  • Once installed, select to activate the plugin.
  • Now your plugin should be installed and ready to use!

In the "Settings" section for the BMT Micro plugin, you will be able to enter your Vendor ID to display your custom BMT cart set up during the checkout process.

Note: The .zip file should appear in your 'Downloads' folder, but might vary depending on your configuration and/or operating system.
