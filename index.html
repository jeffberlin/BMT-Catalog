About Us

BMT Micro is a leader in online e-commerce and electronically deliverable software. We started as a commercial online BBS service in 1992 and made the natural transition to selling online software as we grew and developed with our large customer base. BMT Micro is dedicated to providing the best order processing service possible by offering easy access to customer support, staying ahead of industry standards, and ensuring the highest level of safety for our vendors and their customers. BMT Micro has a global presence, offering a multitude of currencies and language localization, and serves software vendors and developers from all over the world. If you are interested in partnering with us or would like more information please contact vendors@bmtmicro.com.
